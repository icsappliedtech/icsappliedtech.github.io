School

Here's a little bit about my school.

I go to school at International Community School in Bangkok, Thailand. I am in 9th grade, with siblings in 11th, 6th, and 4th grade. My parents both teach full-time at ICS, so my entire family is at the school at least 5 times a week, for most of the year. My mom teaches art for all of elementary: K5 to 5th grade. My dad teaches high school Bible, or JCHL, for 3 grades in high school (10th to 12th), but he mostly teaches 10th graders. ICS is a very community-centered school, making it a loving and safe environment for all students and teachers.
