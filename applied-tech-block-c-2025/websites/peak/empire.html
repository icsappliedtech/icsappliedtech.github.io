Der Kaiserreich

The German Empire.

The current kaiser is Kaiser Jason, the 88th monarch from the House of Weimer. The capital and largest city is Berlin, and the official language is German. The most common religion is Luthern Christianity, with other majorities being Roman Catholicism, and a small populations of Jews.
