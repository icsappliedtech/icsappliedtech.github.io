About!

Here's a little bit about myself.

I am Thai, and I moved to Bangkok when I was in grade 1 because of my parents' business. I studied at Sarasas, a well-known school with many branches across Thailand. In 2024, I transferred to ICS as a 10th grader, which was a big but exciting change for me. I enjoy drawing and often spend my free time listening to music. I can play the guitar, but my favorite instrument is the piano because its sound feels peaceful and expressive. Both music and art are important parts of my life and help me express my creativity.
