    • About Me

    Hey there! I'm Thumb, a high school junior currently studying at ICS Bangkok.

    I currently live in Bangkok, the capital city of Thailand. Bangkok is also one of the 77 provinces of Thailand and is located in the central region of the country. I live in the Watthana district of Bangkok. Watthana or Vadhana is one of the 50 districts (khet) of Bangkok, Thailand. Neighbouring districts are (from the north clockwise): Ratchathewi, Huai Khwang, Suan Luang, Phra Khanong, Khlong Toei, and Pathum Wan.

    I live in the Ekkamai neighborhood of Watthana district. Ekkamai is oftentimes overlooked by visitors to Thailand. With its hard-hitting neighbor, Thonglor, stealing all its potential suitors, this area remains an expat and local favorite but is abandoned by tourists. However, as the COVID-19 pandemic starts to come to an end, visitors to Bangkok have started exploring new areas, like Ekkamai, where newly opened aesthetic cafes blend with Japanese businesses and restaurants.

    In the future, I aspire to become a physician and I desire to study medicine after high school.
