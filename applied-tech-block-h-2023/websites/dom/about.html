    Hello my name is Dom and i am currently in 9th grade and i study at ICS international community school. This is my 4th year in ICS and have several friends in this school. It is really interesting how my whole life completely changed from the transfer from midlle school to Highschool. it seems like that i have more pressure than i had expected. At the beginning of my journey to ICS, my head was covered with dull thoughts that school would be boring and i would have no fun time in this school but it was the complete opposite. yes, school is rather boring and tiring but i shared some fun moments with my friends from school.

    Apart from this i also have some hobbies i like to share with my friends such as playing games, playing badminton,etc. Me and my friends will also go out around 1 time a week to go out and enjoy life, these are one of the big things i will cherish in my heart. Ics is a really fun place to be in and the people here are extremely kind. Even the school work might stress me out, i still enjoy this school.
