    • About Me

    Hello! My name is Nishka. I am 14 years old, in 9th grade, and taking Applied Technology class at ICS this year.

    Dear reader of this information concerning myself: Hello! As Charlotte the spider of "Charlotte's Web" would say, salutations. Im not quite completely sure how to explain myself to you. I'm a jumbled tangle of yarn string that tells my stories and holds together my heart. Things that bring me joy are spending time with family and friends, reading stories, writing stories, and making and hearing music.

    I love the feeling of holding hands with people I love, and letting the sunlight hit my face in the way that feels just right. I try to notice the tatters in leaves and the birds taking flight and the millipedes narrowly escaping death-by-crushing. I bury my head in books, but I don't like the word "book-worm", so I refer to myslef as a "book dragon". It makes me glad to put words on paper, and make ink letters overflow with feelings and descriptions and incredible stories. I overthink too often, and I'm not very sure of myself, but the incredibly incredible people I am blessed to have around me help me grow. Thankyou for taking the time to read this summary of me!
