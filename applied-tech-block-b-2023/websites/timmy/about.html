    • About Me!

    I'm currently a student at ICS.
    My favorite hobby is rock climbing.

    A few months ago, I stumbled upon a new form of exercising: Rock Climbing! Eversince then, I've became committed to the sport. Rock climbing not only uses your physical body, but it also exercises your mind. When bouldering (a form of rock climbing without the use of rope), you must plan out how you'll climb the route you are presented with, before starting the climb. Even if you have the required amount of physical strength, technique and planning also plays a big role in how well you climb. Over the past few months, I've been constantly improving at the sport, trying to climb at least once a week.

    Sadly, I injured my hand while climbing 2 weeks ago. This has been quite an unfortunate event for me since climbing was a big passion of mine. However taking a break from something you normally do can also help you discover new things. Luckily, I believe that I can soon return to climbing within a few days, and hopefully, the 2 week break will not have affected my ability to climb as much as I think it did.
