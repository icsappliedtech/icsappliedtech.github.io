Direction
Terms related to strategy, vision, goals, and leadership. Ask yourself: Does this word describe where the company is going? (e.g., "Pivot," "North Star")
People
Terms related to roles, relationships, communication, and team dynamics. Ask yourself: Is this about who is doing the work or talking? (e.g., "Touch base," "Bandwidth")
Resources
Terms related to time, money, budgets, tools, and assets. Ask yourself: Is this about the things the company spends or uses? (e.g., "Burn rate," "Tech debt")
Process
Terms related to workflows, methods, rules, and daily operations. Ask yourself: Does this describe how the actual work is getting done? (e.g., "Streamline," "Agile")
Results
Terms related to outcomes, metrics, deliverables, and evaluating success. Ask yourself: Is this about measuring what was achieved? (e.g., "Bottom line," "ROI")
